About Us

Two trusted names in landscaping, united by a shared commitment to quality, professionalism, and exceptional outdoor craftsmanship.

Our Story

Landscapia and Walker Landscape & Design have built their reputations on delivering premium outdoor spaces with precision, creativity, and care. Together, we bring decades of combined experience across landscaping, design, construction, and project management.

Our partnership allows us to deliver projects at scale while maintaining the craftsmanship and attention to detail that homeowners and commercial clients expect.

Our Values

Quality

We set high standards for every project, ensuring durability, precision, and long‑lasting results.

Professionalism

Clear communication, reliability, and respect for our clients and subcontractors are at the core of our work.

Craftsmanship

Every outdoor space is built with care, skill, and pride — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Why We Work With Subcontractors

Our subcontractor network allows us to deliver projects efficiently while maintaining the highest standards of workmanship. We collaborate with skilled tradespeople who share our commitment to quality and professionalism.

By working together, we create outdoor spaces that homeowners love — and we support the growth of talented trades across the UK.
